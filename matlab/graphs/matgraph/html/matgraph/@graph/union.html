union

PURPOSE

union(g,h1,h2) --- set g equal to the union of h1 and h2.

SYNOPSIS

DESCRIPTION 

 union(g,h1,h2) --- set g equal to the union of h1 and h2.
 The vertices of g are the union of the vertex sets of h1 and h2, and the
 edge set of g is the union of the edge sets of h1 and h2.
