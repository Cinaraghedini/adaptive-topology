split

PURPOSE

part = split(g) --- partition the vertices of g into two subsets

SYNOPSIS

function part = split(g)

DESCRIPTION 

 part = split(g) --- partition the vertices of g into two subsets
 The graph is partitioned based on the eigenvector associated with the 2nd
 smallest eigenvalue of the Laplacian of g.
